Top Attractions

Here are some must‑see places when visiting Ghana:

Cape Coast Castle

Explore one of the most significant slave trade forts on the West African coast—a moving experience and UNESCO World Heritage site.

Kakum National Park

Walk among the treetops on the famous canopy walkway and discover diverse flora and fauna in the rainforest.

Mole National Park

Ghana’s premier wildlife park—spot elephants, antelopes, baboons and over 300 bird species in the savannah.

Beaches of Accra

Relax at Labadi and Kokrobite beaches—great for music, food, and sunsets.
